# Android & iOS Troubleshooting

## Slow Device

- Restart device
- Free storage
- Update OS
- Remove unused apps
- Clear app cache

---

## Battery Drain

- Reduce brightness
- Disable unused background apps
- Enable battery saver
- Check battery health

---

## Wi-Fi Issues

- Toggle Wi-Fi
- Forget and reconnect
- Restart router
- Reset network settings

---

## Bluetooth Issues

- Restart Bluetooth
- Remove paired device
- Pair again
- Update software

---

## App Crashes

- Force stop app
- Clear cache
- Update app
- Reinstall app

---

## No Storage

- Delete unnecessary files
- Move photos to cloud
- Clear downloads
- Remove unused apps
